## Orders on hold settings

Use this tab to pause fulfillment while address issues are unresolved.

## How to configure

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Open **Orders on hold**.
3. Turn hold behavior on.
4. Choose whether to set hold on unresolved issues.
5. Choose whether to auto-release after correction.
6. Define your hold reasons.
7. Click **Save settings**.

## Controls explained

- **Set hold when unresolved issue is detected**
- **Auto-release hold after correction**
- **Hold reasons**: custom values your team can track

<Info>
This works best together with post-checkout validation, because post-checkout determines unresolved issue state.
</Info>

## FAQ

### When should I enable order holds?

Enable holds when unresolved address issues must be reviewed before fulfillment.

### What does auto-release hold do?

It removes the hold automatically after a successful correction flow.

### Are hold reasons mandatory?

They are strongly recommended so teams and automations understand why an order is paused.
