## Dashboard metrics

When you open AddressPill, you see 4 main numbers:

- **Protected checkouts**: how many checkouts were validated
- **Address issues**: how many address problems were detected
- **Delivery value saved**: estimated value saved by avoiding failed deliveries
- **Validation cost**: your validation-related spend

These numbers help you understand impact at a glance.

## Quick actions

Under the metrics, you see **Quick Actions**:

- **In-checkout validation**
- **Post-checkout validation**

Use these shortcuts to open the most important setup areas quickly.

## Sidebar areas (simple explanation)

- **Settings**: where you configure all validation behavior
- **Support & Billing**: support and plan management

## Settings tabs you should know

Inside **Settings**, you can configure:

1. **In-checkout validation**
2. **Post-checkout validation**
3. **Packing stations / PO Boxes**
4. **Tag orders**
5. **Orders on hold**
6. **Automations**

Each of these is explained in the **Features** and **Settings** sections of this documentation.

## FAQ

### What are the four dashboard metrics in AddressPill?

Protected checkouts, Address issues, Delivery value saved, and Validation cost.

### Where can I configure all validation behavior?

In the **Settings** area of AddressPill.

### Which settings tabs are most important for beginners?

Start with In-checkout validation, Post-checkout validation, Tag orders, and Orders on hold.
