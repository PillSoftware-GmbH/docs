## In-checkout validation setup

In-checkout validation checks the shipping address **inside Shopify checkout**, before the customer places the order.

It helps you stop invalid addresses early.

## Step-by-step

1. In AddressPill, open **Settings**.
2. If you do not have a plan yet, choose a plan first.
3. Turn on **In-checkout validation**.
4. Enable the checkout rules you want.
5. Click **Save settings**.
6. In Shopify admin, open **Settings** (bottom-left).
7. Open **Checkout**.
8. Scroll down to **Checkout rules**.
9. Open **AddressPill Checkout Validation**.
10. Click **Turn on**.
11. Select **Allow all customers to complete checkout**.
12. Click **Save**.

## Result

In-checkout validation is now active for your store.

Customers get address feedback during checkout based on your selected rules.

## FAQ

### What does in-checkout validation do in Shopify?

It validates the shipping address during checkout before the customer places the order.

### Why do I need both AddressPill settings and Shopify Checkout rules?

AddressPill defines the rules, and Shopify Checkout rules activates the checkout validation extension.

### Which Shopify option should I select when turning it on?

Select **Allow all customers to complete checkout**, then save.
