## Post-checkout validation setup

Post-checkout validation runs **after checkout**.

It checks addresses on the **Thank You** and **Order Status** pages.

This is useful if you want lower checkout friction while still catching issues.

## Step-by-step

1. In AddressPill, open **Settings**.
2. Open the **Post-checkout validation** tab.
3. If you do not have an active subscription, choose a plan.
4. Turn on **Post-checkout validation**.
5. Enable the post-checkout features you want.
6. Click **Save settings**.
7. In Shopify admin, open **Online Store**.
8. Open **Themes** and click **Edit theme**.
9. In the top bar, switch page selector to **Checkout and customer accounts**.
10. Click **Apps** (top-left).
11. Add **AddressPill thank-you-validation** with the plus button.
12. Select **Thank you**.
13. Switch to **All**, click plus again, and select **Order status**.
14. Save theme changes.

## Result

Post-checkout validation is now active for this shop.

## FAQ

### What does post-checkout validation do in Shopify?

It validates addresses after purchase on Thank You and Order Status pages.

### Why is the app block required in the theme editor?

The app block is what displays and runs the post-checkout validation experience for customers.

### Which pages must include the AddressPill app block?

Both **Thank you** and **Order status**.
