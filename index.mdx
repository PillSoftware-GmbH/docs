## What AddressPill does

AddressPill helps you avoid shipping errors in Shopify.

It checks customer addresses at two moments:

- **In-checkout validation**: before the order is placed
- **Post-checkout validation**: after the order is placed

This means you can catch problems early, reduce manual work, and protect fulfillment quality.

## FAQ

### What is AddressPill for Shopify?

AddressPill is a Shopify app that validates shipping addresses before and after checkout to reduce delivery errors.

### What is the difference between in-checkout and post-checkout validation?

In-checkout runs before payment. Post-checkout runs after payment on Thank You and Order Status pages.
